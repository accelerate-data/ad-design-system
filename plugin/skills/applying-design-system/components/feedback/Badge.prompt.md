Compact pill for status, tiers, versions, and counts. Soft tinted background with a saturated label.

```jsx
<Badge variant="success" icon={<i className="ph ph-check-circle" />}>Production</Badge>
<Badge variant="warning">Pending review</Badge>
<Badge variant="pacific">v2.4.1</Badge>
```

Variants: brand (`pacific`/`seafoam`/`navy`), semantic (`success`/`warning`/`error`/`info`), and `neutral`. Keep labels to one or two words.
